What is Quarterly Projects?


IEEE Quarterly Projects is your path to a great project experience. Over the course of 10 weeks (4 - 5 hours per week), you will be working with other students and an assigned mentor to create a truly AMAZING hardware/software product! Similar to an internship or job setting, there will be multiple opportunities to gain hands on experience that can be incredible milestones for your career. We also offer bi-weekly work-a-thons with exciting workshops like arduino, web & mobile development, SolidWorks, and more. We want to know that you have the motivation, reliability and initiative required to develop a product from START to FINISH. Don't worry, our mentors will provide you with all the help you need along the way!



What is QP++?


This quarter we are bringing back QP++ - the next iteration of Quarterly Projects - for those looking to challenge themselves! Unlike Quarterly Projects, you will compete in teams of 5 - 6 for 10 weeks to develop a much more technically complex product. You will receive a larger budget, advanced workshops and you will learn relevant skills to solve technically demanding problems in the future!
