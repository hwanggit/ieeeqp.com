Quick Links

  • Explore this site!

    Feel free to check out links like the team page, mentors page, and documentation to get a better idea of what resources are availabe to you!

    Go to the IEEE Project Lounge on the 4th floor of Jacobs Hall to get help from mentors, replace hardware parts, use soldering machines, 3D printers, and more! There will be officers there constantly to guide you!

Upcoming Events
6
OCT

Deadline to Submit Application (CLOSED)!

This is the final deadline after the GBM in which students will be able to submit QP and QP++ applications. Once this deadline has passed, mentors will begin sending out application acceptances via email within a week.

11
OCT

QP and QP++ Kickoff

At the kickoff participants meet their teams, receive the entry packet, get informed about hardware, prizes, and the theme of the overall competition. We will also introduce the mentors, and set up lines of communication so you can stay in contact!

19
OCT

First QP/QP++ Workathon

3pm - 7pm (Qualcomm Conference Room EBU 1)

NOTE: If you couldn't come to the kickoff Friday, please come out to Saturday's workathon to be onboarded!

Every two weeks on Saturday or Sunday, QP/QP++ will hold a workathon session for teams to work on their project! Mentors will be there to check up on you and you will have all the hardware resources you need. There is also free food provided!
