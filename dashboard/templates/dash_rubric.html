  2. Rubric

QP Rubric

1 = zero effort was put into the application

2 = some of the sections are filled out to the best of ability

3 = less than half of sections are filled out to the best of ability

4 = roughly half of sections are filled out to the best of ability

5 = more than half of sections are filled out to the best of ability

6 = almost all sections are filled out well, but applicant will not work well in a group setting

7 = all sections are filled out well, but applicant might not work well in a group setting

8 = all sections are filled out well, and applicant will probably work well in a group setting

9 = all sections are filled out well, and applicant will thrive best in a group setting

10 = all sections are filled out well, and the applicant displays outstanding potential for leading their team

QP++ Rubric*

1 = zero effort was put into the application

2 = some of the sections are filled out to the best of ability

3 = less than half of sections are filled out to the best of ability

4 = roughly half of sections are filled out to the best of ability

5 = more than half of sections are filled out to the best of ability

6 = almost all sections are filled out well, but applicant has no technical expertise

7 = all sections are filled out well, but applicant has little technical expertise

8 = all sections are filled out well, and applicant has decent technical expertise

9 = all sections are filled out well, and applicant has proficient technical expertise

10 = all sections are filled out well, and the applicant has outstanding technical expertise

*If they applied for both, use QP++ rubric
